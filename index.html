Overview

Kolhapur district is home to several esteemed engineering colleges that offer a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering and technology. This webpage provides a list of notable institutions that cater to aspiring engineers.

List of Engineering Colleges

Shivaji University, Kolhapur

Offers a range of engineering programs with modern facilities and experienced faculty.

Government College of Engineering, Karad

A premier engineering institution providing quality education and research opportunities.

Padm. Dr. D. Y. Patil Institute of Engineering and Technology

Provides undergraduate and postgraduate engineering programs with excellent campus facilities.

Rajarshi Shahu College of Engineering

Known for its strong emphasis on practical learning and industry collaborations.

Shri Sant Gajanan Maharaj College of Engineering

Focuses on developing technical skills through innovative teaching methods.

Jawaharlal Nehru Engineering College

Offers a variety of engineering disciplines with a commitment to excellence in education.
